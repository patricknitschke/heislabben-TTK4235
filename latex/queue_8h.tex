Identifies when to serve a customer and serves a customer if certain conditions are met. 

If any of the below conditons are satisfied, \mbox{\hyperlink{queue_8h_a51e76147f4af2a6ee29645d6792fbc69}{queue\+\_\+clear\+\_\+orders\+\_\+at\+\_\+floor}} is called to remove orders and turn off lights at the elevator\textquotesingle{}s current floor.

Condtions to which a customer can be served\+: \begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Direction is up and same direction order exists \item Direction is up and no orders above \item Direction is down and same direction order exists \item Direction is down and no orders below \item Last order in {\ttfamily queue}.\end{DoxyItemize}
\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
1 if the conditions to serve a customer are met. 0 otherwise. 
\end{DoxyReturn}
\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}

\end{DoxyNote}
